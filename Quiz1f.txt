> Why is it working?
	
	-The raspberry pi is already working because of the new operating system that has been inistalled in the microcomputer.
	-The external peripherals connected to the microcomputer were all performing correctly in a way that those peripherals meet the specified value needed for the raspberry pi to work properly.
	-If the LED in the microcomputer blinks with a color green and with a steady red LED, it means that the power supply were working right and the operating system installed were done and good.
